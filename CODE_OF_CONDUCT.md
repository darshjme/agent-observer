# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, experience level, or identity.

## Our Standards

**Encouraged:**
- Respectful, constructive feedback
- Welcoming newcomers
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Not tolerated:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behaviour
- Publishing others' private information

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported to darshjme@gmail.com. Maintainers will review and respond appropriately, up to and including permanent bans.
